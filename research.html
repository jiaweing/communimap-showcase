Research Synthesis

Our research reveals that successful behavior change systems must balance simplicity with meaningfulness. Users need to feel that their actions matter, understand how tracking works, and be part of a community working toward shared goals.

Key Finding: The most effective approach combines accurate CO‚ÇÇ calculations with social features and clear progress visualization, while maintaining transparency about methodology and data use.
