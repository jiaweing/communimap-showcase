Project Overview

Making CO‚ÇÇ tracking fun while helping communities walk and bike more

The Problem

CO‚ÇÇ tracking apps are super boring. Most of them just show you numbers or put you on a leaderboard competing with random people. That doesn't make anyone excited about helping the environment - it just makes you feel guilty or competitive about the wrong things.

üî¨

What We Studied

We looked into what makes people tick when it comes to games and environmental stuff. Turns out, growing virtual trees and forests is way more engaging than competing on leaderboards. Who knew?

üöÄ

Our Solution

We made a forest planting game! Instead of boring numbers and rankings, your walking and cycling journeys help grow beautiful virtual trees. It takes the data from communiMap and turns it into something you actually want to engage with.

About GALLANT

GALLANT (Glasgow as a Living Lab Accelerating Novel Transformation) is a University of Glasgow project that's trying to help with climate change solutions. We're part of the Movement Co-Lab, which is all about getting people to walk and bike more - and making sure they can do it safely and joyfully.
