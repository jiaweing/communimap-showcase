About GALLANT

GALLANT (Glasgow as a Living Lab Accelerating Novel Transformation) is a University of Glasgow project designing innovative climate adaptation solutions. Our work focuses on promoting active travel through the Movement Co-Lab, exploring how communities move through their environments safely and joyfully.
