Collective Team Reflection

As a team, we discovered that our 3-week constraint became our greatest strength. It forced us to:

  • Prioritize ruthlessly: Focus only on features that directly addressed user needs
  • Collaborate intensively: Daily communication became essential for rapid iteration
  • Embrace imperfection: Ship a working solution rather than wait for perfection
  • Trust the process: Lean on each other's expertise to make quick, informed decisions
  • Learn continuously: Every day brought new insights that shaped our final solution

The compressed timeline taught us that innovation doesn't always need months of development - sometimes the best solutions emerge when you're forced to focus on what truly matters.
